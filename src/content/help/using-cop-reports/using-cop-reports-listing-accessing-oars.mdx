Watch the short video below to see how to use the Command Brief to filter and view OARs.

<Video source={'https://product.cop.homeoffice.gov.uk/videos/Command_Brief%20OAR%20Video.mp4'} width={487} height={64}/>

<BreakLine pixels={30} />

To change the date range either drag the circles or click on and select specific start and end dates.

Filter the OARs by selecting the Branch (Region),  Command, Location and/or Team.

The main panel on the left shows all the OARs submitted up to the last 30 minutes.

The smaller panels on the right show <u>for your selected list</u> of OARs:

a. the last 5 OARS containing positive and/or negative search results;<br />
b. the staff numbers and total hours on the last 5 shifts;<br />
c. overall time in PCP, Customs, Detection/Search, Cyclamen and Other activities in the last 7 days.

<Image source={"/images/help/using-cop-reports/Command_OAR screen.png"} alt={"command oar screen"} />

<BreakLine pixels={15} />

You can toggle between the Command Brief views for OARs (Command Brief – OAR) and Border Events (Command Brief – IEN) by clicking these options.

To open an OAR listed in the main panel select the ‘COP url’ link on the left, the OAR can be viewed in the **‘Cases’** part of COP, this will open in a new window.

To view the OAR follow the three-part process shown below – **Note you will not submit a new OAR and the data displayed cannot be changed**
