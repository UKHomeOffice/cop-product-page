<MediumTitle text={'Command Brief'} />

Command Brief lists all OARs and Border Events submitted to COP.

The list of OARs and Border Events can be filtered by Date,Branch (Region) and Location, OARS can also be filtered by Command and Team.

OARs and Border Events are updated every 30 minutes (on the hour and at half past the hour). 

You can view the content of any OAR or Border Event listed.
