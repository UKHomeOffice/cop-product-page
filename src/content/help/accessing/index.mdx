You will need to create an account on COP before you can access the various services and forms (such as Man Dec and OAR).

You can do this with your POISE Active Directory (AD) account.

<MediumTitle text={'Create your COP account'}/>



**1.** &nbsp;<a class="govuk-link" href="https://www.cop.homeoffice.gov.uk/" target="_blank">Go to COP</a> and sign in using the **‘POISE’** button.

<ul class="govuk-list govuk-list--bullet">
  <li>You should sign in to COP automatically if your laptop/desktop is already logged into <strong>POISE.</strong> If on a mobile, you’ll need to enter your <strong>POISE</strong> username and password again when prompted.</li>
  <li>Laptops/desktops need Windows 10 and an up-to-date <strong>Microsoft Edge browser.</strong></li>
</ul>

<div style={{marginBottom: "20px"}}>
    <Image source={'/images/help/accessing/1.png'} alt={'Enter_Submit'}/>
</div>




**2.** &nbsp; After reading and accepting the privacy notice, enter your details in the ‘Onboard User’ form.

<ul class="govuk-list govuk-list--bullet">
  <li>Include your <strong>work mobile phone number</strong></li>
  <li>You will also need your <strong>Adelphi number</strong> (ask your line manager if unsure)</li>
</ul>

<div style={{marginBottom: "20px"}}>
    <Image source={'/images/help/accessing/2.png'} alt={'Enter_Submit'}/>
</div>


**3.** &nbsp; Once you submit your details, you'll have access to COP. **Click the link available** to access the various services. (Note: You may need to close your browser and sign in again if you want to use COP immediately)

<div style={{marginBottom: "20px"}}>
    <Image source={'/images/help/accessing/3.png'} alt={'OAR_Submit'}/>
</div>
