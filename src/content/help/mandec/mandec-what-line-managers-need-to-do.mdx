<MediumTitle text={'What Line Managers need to do'}/>

If you’re a line manager and need to review a staff members Man Dec, you’ll get an email notifying you about this. email

You can see under ‘tasks assigned to you’ in the Operation Dashboard who’s submitted a Man Dec for you to review.

<MediumTitle text={'Integrity Lead'}/>

If you need further guidance on something your staff member has declared, you can refer the query to your integrity lead.

When the integrity lead responds, you'll get an email notification and be able to view the response in your task list.

<MediumTitle text={'Checking report completion'}/>

Once you have approved a staff declaration, you can see the date of when the Man Dec was completed in “Reports.” It will also allow you to see who amongst your staff has completed the form. If their name hasn’t come up then they haven’t registered.
