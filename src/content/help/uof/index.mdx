You must complete a Use of Force (UoF) form when you <Link class="govuk-link" to="/help/uof/uof-when-you-must-report-use-of-force/">use force against a person.</Link>

It is **compulsory** to complete as soon as an event takes place.

The UoF form allows Border Force to keep a record of any events. It:
