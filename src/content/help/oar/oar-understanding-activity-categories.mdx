<MediumTitle text={'Understand activity categories'} />

Select a transport mode worked on by the team.  All activities are entered under each of the transport modes worked on during the shift.

Where activities cannot be assigned to a transport mode then put these under the usual or main transport mode that applies to a team or at a Port.

Enter the activities the team undertook in the shift and the time spent on each activity. Note, travel time can be recorded as a separate activity.

Make sure you choose the correct activity category. For further clarification about each transport mode or activity open the relevant link below:
