<MediumTitle text={'Add shift debrief notes'} />

To add ‘narrative’ shift debrief notes select ‘Yes’ to this option before you submit the OAR, a text box will open into which you can optionally type shift handover or other notes.  

When you have submitted the OAR a receipt e-mail will be sent to you. 

The e-mail contains a link to the completed OAR you submitted including any shift debrief notes.
